Certifications & Quality Assurance

Committed to the highest standards of quality, safety, and compliance

Our Certifications

At Organiya Global, quality and safety are paramount. We maintain multiple certifications and conduct rigorous testing to ensure our superfoods meet the highest standards for export and distribution worldwide.

FSSAI Certification

Our processing facilities are FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) certified, ensuring compliance with Indian food safety and standards regulations. This certification validates our commitment to safe, hygienic processing and quality control.

  • Food-safe processing facilities
  • Hygiene and sanitation compliance
  • Regular facility inspections
  • Regulatory compliance verified

Lab Testing & Quality Reports

Every batch of our products undergoes comprehensive laboratory testing by third-party certified labs. Tests include purity analysis, heavy metal screening, microbial safety, nutritional content verification, and potency assessment.

  • Third-party certified lab testing
  • Heavy metals screening
  • Microbial safety testing
  • Nutritional content verification
  • Potency and purity analysis
  • Lab reports provided with shipments

Organic Growing Methods

Our superfoods are grown using organic farming practices and sustainable agricultural methods. We work with farmers who follow natural cultivation techniques without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, preserving soil health and environmental sustainability.

  • Sustainable farming practices
  • No synthetic pesticides or fertilizers
  • Soil health preservation
  • Environmental sustainability
  • Farmer training and support

HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points)

Our processing facilities follow HACCP principles to identify, evaluate, and control food safety hazards. This systematic preventive approach ensures food safety throughout the production process.

  • Systematic hazard identification
  • Critical control point monitoring
  • Food safety management system
  • Process control documentation

Export Certifications

For international exports, we provide all necessary documentation including phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, commercial invoices, and compliance documentation as required by destination countries.

  • Phytosanitary certificates
  • Certificate of origin
  • Commercial invoices
  • Packing lists
  • Country-specific compliance docs

Batch Traceability

Every product batch is assigned a unique lot number, enabling full traceability from farm to final shipment. This allows us to track and verify product origin, processing date, and quality control records.

  • Unique lot numbers for each batch
  • Farm-to-shipment tracking
  • Quality control records
  • Processing date verification
