About Organiya Global LLP

Premium Indian Superfoods • Global Export Capabilities • Trusted by Wellness Brands Worldwide

Our Story

Organiya Global LLP was founded with a simple mission: to bring India's rich heritage of natural nutrition to the world. Our journey began on family-owned farms where we have grown vegetables, fruits, and herbs using clean and sustainable methods for years. As global demand for plant-based and functional nutrition increased, we expanded our focus to export-grade Moringa, Turmeric, Ashwagandha, and premium superfoods trusted by health-conscious consumers and wellness brands around the world.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading global supplier of India's pure, natural, and plant-powered superfoods, helping people live healthier lives with clean nutrition.

Our Mission

• Deliver export-grade superfoods with consistent quality

• Support sustainable farming practices across India

• Empower global wellness brands with clean natural ingredients

• Maintain transparency and trust across our supply chain

Rooted in India, Serving the World

Our farms are located in nutrient-rich regions of Southern and Western India, known for producing some of the world's highest-quality herbs and superfoods. We combine traditional agricultural knowledge with modern processing methods to preserve purity, color, taste, and nutritional density.
