Moringa: India's Most Powerful Superfood

Nature's multivitamin with exceptional nutritional density

Why Moringa?

Moringa oleifera, often called the "Miracle Tree," is one of the most nutrient-dense plants on Earth. Our premium Moringa powder is sourced from sustainable Indian farms and processed with care to preserve its exceptional nutritional profile, making it ideal for supplements, functional foods, and wellness products.

Nutritional Benefits

High in Vitamins A, C, E

Contains 7x more vitamin C than oranges, 4x more vitamin A than carrots, and significant vitamin E for antioxidant support.

Iron, Calcium & Plant Protein

Rich in iron (more than spinach), calcium (4x more than milk), and complete plant protein with all 9 essential amino acids.

Natural Anti-inflammatory

Contains powerful antioxidants like quercetin and chlorogenic acid that help reduce inflammation and support overall wellness.

Energy & Immunity Support

Natural energy boost from B vitamins and iron, plus immune system support from vitamin C and antioxidants.

Applications & Usage

For Supplements

Our fine-powder Moringa (80-120 mesh) is perfect for capsule filling, tablet manufacturing, and supplement formulations. The consistent particle size ensures uniform distribution in products.

For Functional Foods

Ideal for smoothie powders, protein bars, energy drinks, baked goods, and wellness food products. Moringa adds nutritional value without overpowering flavors when used in appropriate quantities.

For Beverages

Perfect for green smoothies, health drinks, tea blends, and functional beverages. Moringa powder mixes well and provides a natural green color with minimal flavor impact.

For Retail Products

Our Moringa is available in retail-ready packaging or bulk formats for your brand. We offer private label options and custom packaging to meet your market needs.
